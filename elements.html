Programs

Choose the depth that fits your goals. Every pathway starts with a Pre‑Check so the testing and plan are proportional, clinically sensible, and actionable.

Pre‑Check

A short intake + goal review that clarifies your priorities (longevity, brain health, metabolic health, performance, recovery). You receive a recommended plan and pricing overview before committing to deeper testing.

Baseline diagnostics

Three tiers—standalone. We recommend the minimum tier that still changes decisions.

Essential baseline

  • Core bloodwork + cardiometabolic risk markers
  • Metabolic health (glucose/insulin dynamics), inflammation, thyroid
  • Liver/kidney, iron status, key micronutrients
  • Action plan with priorities for the next 12 weeks

Advanced baseline

  • Everything in Essential, plus deeper lipid/apolipoprotein profiling and expanded metabolic markers
  • Body composition context and recovery/sleep signal integration
  • Optional targeted add‑ons when decision‑changing (e.g., CGM coaching, stool testing)

Elite baseline

  • Everything in Advanced, plus specialist add‑ons coordinated through partner networks where appropriate
  • Higher‑frequency follow‑up, concierge coordination, and multi‑domain optimization (brain, metabolic, gut, sleep)
  • Designed for executives, athletes, and clients who want maximum clarity and support

12‑week precision program

A structured intervention cycle built on your baseline. You receive weekly priorities, accountability, and iterative adjustments based on real‑world response.

  • Nutrition and metabolic coaching
  • Training, recovery, and injury‑risk reduction
  • Sleep architecture and stress physiology
  • Evidence‑based supplementation (when it improves outcomes)
  • Repeat measures at defined checkpoints

Membership

For clients who want long‑term compounding: periodic re‑testing, continuous refinement, and concierge coordination across providers.

Important: BetterHealth is not emergency care and does not replace your primary care physician. We do not promise cures. Any partner‑delivered testing (e.g., imaging or neuro‑physiology) is coordinated based on suitability and availability.
