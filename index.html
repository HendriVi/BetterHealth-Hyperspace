What we do

We focus on the highest‑leverage systems that drive long‑term health: brain, metabolism, sleep, gut, and recovery—supported by diagnostics and repeat measurement.

BetterHealth offers preventative care and lifestyle support that’s practical, measurable, and tailored to you. Whether you’re managing a concern or simply want a stronger baseline, we help you build the habits and routines that move the needle—without the overwhelm.

Diagnostics‑first risk review

Advanced labs and clinical context to surface hidden constraints: lipids/ApoB, insulin resistance, inflammation, thyroid, micronutrients, liver/kidney markers, and more—tailored to you.

Brain health & cognition

Objective assessment of brain function and drivers (sleep, stress physiology, metabolic health). When appropriate, we coordinate additional neuro‑testing through partner networks.

Metabolic & body composition

Glucose dynamics, appetite regulation, muscle preservation, and cardiometabolic fitness—using practical nutrition, movement, and recovery protocols you can sustain.

Gut, inflammation & immune balance

Symptoms are rarely isolated. We address GI function, dietary triggers, and inflammatory load, with escalation to targeted stool testing when it changes decisions.

Sleep & stress physiology

Sleep is a multiplier. We improve sleep architecture and recovery capacity, and reduce chronic stress load with evidence‑based behavioral and physiological interventions.

Programs, workshops & corporate

Structured 12‑week programs, executive/athlete support, and corporate education—always tied back to measurable outcomes.
