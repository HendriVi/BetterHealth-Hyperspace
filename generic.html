Our method

BetterHealth is a physician‑led precision longevity practice. We reduce uncertainty by measuring high‑signal biomarkers, interpreting them in clinical context, and running targeted interventions that are re‑checked over time.

1) Pre‑Check

A short intake that clarifies your goals, history, and constraints. You receive a recommended diagnostic depth and a clear plan for next steps.

2) Baseline Diagnostic Day

We establish your baseline: advanced bloodwork, cardiometabolic markers, body composition, and lifestyle/behavioral mapping. When appropriate, we coordinate add‑on testing through partner networks (e.g., brain physiology, microbiome, imaging).

3) 12‑week intervention cycle

We translate data into action: nutrition, training and recovery, sleep, stress physiology, and evidence‑based supplementation. You get a structured plan, accountability, and adjustments based on how your body responds.

4) Membership follow‑up

Long‑term health compounds. Membership keeps you on track with periodic re‑testing, course corrections, and concierge coordination with your treating clinicians when needed.

What this looks like in practice

  • Measure: Choose tests that change decisions (not novelty panels).
  • Interpret: Clinical review + context (symptoms, meds, schedule, stress load).
  • Intervene: Targeted plan with clear priorities and constraints.
  • Iterate: Re‑check and adjust—so results are objective.

Important: BetterHealth is not emergency care and does not replace your primary care physician. We do not promise cures. We work alongside existing care and focus on prevention, performance, and risk reduction.
